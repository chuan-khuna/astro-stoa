# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Personal reader — the person who built this and returns to it regularly. Context is reflective: a quiet moment, not a search session. The site functions as a commonplace book: a curated record of words that settled, revisited at the reader's own pace.

## Product Purpose

Meditations is a curated quotes site — a place to encounter well-chosen words without noise. It exists as a personal record, not a directory or aggregator. Success means a reader can open it, read something, and feel the design has gotten out of the way.

## Brand Personality

Considered. Unhurried. Precise.

The voice is editorial without being cold: each quote is presented as if it was chosen rather than scraped. The interface is present enough to signal curation but invisible enough to let the words dominate.

## Anti-references

- **Quote aggregator sites (BrainyQuote, Goodreads)**: cluttered, ad-heavy, search-box-first, quantity over selection.
- **Wellness / mindfulness apps**: soft gradients, rounded cards, motivational-poster energy, pastel palettes.
- **Dark-mode dev tools**: monospace, terminal-coded, cold-gray palette — wrong register entirely.

## Design Principles

1. **The words are the interface.** Typography is not decoration; it is the primary affordance. Every spacing and sizing decision should serve legibility and rhythm, not visual interest.
2. **Curation over collection.** The design should feel like something was chosen, not generated. Restraint is signal, not absence.
3. **Nothing earns its place by being common.** Avoid patterns that signal "quote site" or "editorial blog" generically — the anti-references are the first reflex to refuse.
4. **Calm is the default state.** No urgency, no calls to action, no metrics. The emotional register is stillness.
5. **Precision over warmth-by-default.** Warmth comes from the words and typography, not from a warm-tinted background or soft UI treatments.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Body text must hit ≥4.5:1 contrast against the paper background. Reduced-motion alternatives required for any animation. Keyboard navigation must reach all interactive elements.
